We Encourage:

Dancing

Dance is a performing art form consisting of sequences of movement with aesthetic and often symbolic value, either improvised or purposefully selected.

Music

Music is generally defined as the art of arranging sound to create some combination of form, harmony, melody, rhythm or otherwise expressive content

Singing

Singing is the act of creating musical sounds with the voice. A person who sings is called a singer, artist or vocalist (in jazz and/or popular music).

Painting

Painting is the expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities, in a two-dimensional visual language.

Anchoring

Anchoring involves orchestrating the flow of the events, holding the attention of the audience with wit , humour and smart use of crisp and well modulated speech.

Photography

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating durable images by recording light, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film.
